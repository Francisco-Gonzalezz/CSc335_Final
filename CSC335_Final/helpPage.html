How To Play!

  • You have 6 tries to correctly guess the Wordle
    • 7 tries on difficult mode
    • 8 tries on extreme mode
  • Each guess must be a valid word
  • The colors of the letters will change to show you how close your guess was to the actual Wordle

Examples:

S I L L Y

"S" is in the correct spot

D A I L Y

"A" is in the word, but in the wrong spot

V A G U E

"A" is not in the word

A S D F G

The word is not valid
